Keys to Success

🏌️ Golf & Pickleball Days

  • These count as active days — don't add gym on same day
  • Always warm up 10 min before play
  • Stretch hips, shoulder, thoracic after each game
  • Hydrate generously before, during, and after

⚖️ Weight Management Tips

  • Prioritize protein at every meal (0.7–1g per lb bodyweight)
  • Aim for 7–8 hours of sleep — critical for fat loss
  • Stay hydrated: 64+ oz water daily
  • Consistency beats intensity — show up every week

🧘 Flexibility Guidelines

  • Hold stretches 30–45 seconds — never bounce
  • Stretch warm muscles (after workout or warm-up)
  • Focus on hips, hamstrings, thoracic, shoulders
  • Daily gentle movement keeps joints healthy

💪 Core Principles

  • Core = stability, not just crunches
  • Anti-rotation and balance work are essential
  • Engage core during every exercise
  • Strong core = better golf swing and court agility

🛡️ Safety First

  • Always warm up 5–10 min before strength work
  • Stop if you feel sharp or unusual pain
  • Never skip a deload week — recovery is training
  • Consult your doctor if new symptoms arise
